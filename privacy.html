Privacy Policy

This website (harisajmal.com) is maintained by Haris Ajmal as a personal portfolio site. This privacy policy explains how visitor data is handled and your rights as a user.

1. What Information Is Collected

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data to understand how visitors interact with the site. This includes browser type, device, country, pages visited, and duration of visits. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2. Cookies

Only analytics cookies are used. These help us understand traffic patterns and improve the site. No cookies are used for ad targeting, personalization, or remarketing. Consent is explicitly requested via a cookie banner for visitors in the EU and UK.

3. How Data Is Used

  • Monitor site performance and engagement
  • Make improvements based on anonymized insights
  • Ensure a smooth user experience

4. Your Rights

If you're visiting from the EU, UK, or a jurisdiction with privacy laws like GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Refuse cookies by declining consent via the banner
  • Withdraw consent at any time by clearing browser cookies
  • Request deletion of any data (though none is stored directly by this website)

5. Third-Party Sharing

No data is sold or shared with any third parties, except with Google Analytics for the purposes outlined above. Google does not use this data for personalized ads on this site.

6. Contact

For any concerns or privacy-related requests, please contact me directly at contact@harisajmal.com.
